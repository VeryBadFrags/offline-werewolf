Offline Werewolf

Roles

Werewolf

Kill 1 player at night. Win the game by having a villager voted against during the day.

Cultist

You win along with the Werewolves.

Detective

Find the alignement of a player.

Villager

There is nothing special about you.

Bodyguard

Protect a player during the night. Neither you or him can be killed.

Jailer

Prevent a player from using his action during the night.

Jester

If you are exiled during the day, you are the only winner of the game. You also win with the Villagers.
